## What Was Built

### cogs/polls.py — PollsCog

New cog with 8 slash commands under `/poll` group and a reaction enforcement listener:

- `/poll create` — immediate poll with `Xm/Xh/Xd` duration parsing
- `/poll schedule` — one-time or weekly (repeat="weekly") scheduled poll; supports ISO and natural-language datetime parsing
- `/poll schedule_weekly` — recurring weekly poll inserted into `recurring_polls` table
- `/poll results` — live vote counts with 10-char `██░░` percentage bars (ephemeral)
- `/poll close` — manual close; posts results embed with winner(s) bolded
- `/poll list` — all active/scheduled/recurring polls in one embed
- `/poll cancel` — cancels asyncio tasks, optionally deactivates recurring series (Manage Guild)
- `/poll edit_recurring` — updates recurring poll schedule via UPDATE_RECURRING_POLL (Manage Guild)

**Reaction handler (on_raw_reaction_add):**
- Unlimited mode: INSERT OR IGNORE on each reaction
- Radio button (max_choices=1): swaps old reaction/vote for new one atomically
- Max-N mode: removes excess reaction and posts a channel warning (delete_after=10s)

**Background tasks:**
- `_run_close_at`: sleeps until close_at then calls `_close_poll`
- `_run_post_then_close_task`: sleeps until post_at, posts embed, sleeps until close_at, closes
- `_run_weekly_post_task`: indefinite loop — computes next weekday slot, posts, closes, repeats; handles missed slots on restart by posting immediately

**cog_load restart recovery:**
- Re-arms `_close_tasks` for all active polls with a discord_message_id
- Re-arms `_weekly_tasks` for all active recurring polls
